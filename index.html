About Me

Hi, I'm Yuvraj Dave 👋

I'm a backend developer who spends an unhealthy amount of time convincing APIs that 500 Internal Server Error is not a personality trait. Fortunately, they usually listen.

I enjoy building fast, scalable, and secure backend systems that solve real-world problems. Whether it's designing RESTful APIs, optimizing PostgreSQL queries that somehow turned into accidental stress tests, or making authentication behave exactly as it should, I genuinely enjoy the challenge of making systems reliable and efficient.

My primary stack includes Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy 2.0, Alembic, Redis, and uv for modern, high-performance development workflows. I'm passionate about writing clean, maintainable code, implementing secure authentication, and designing backend architectures that are built to scale.

Beyond coding, I'm constantly learning about system design, distributed systems, database optimization, and backend best practices. I enjoy exploring open-source projects, experimenting with new technologies, and sharpening my problem-solving skills—because there's always a better query to write or a cleaner architecture to build.

"I believe great backend development isn't just about making things work—it's about making them work efficiently, securely, and well enough that the frontend developer doesn't have to message me with 'Hey... is the API down?' 😄"
